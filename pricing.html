Can my landlord evict me without notice?

General legal information

Across the UK, the answer depends on the kind of tenancy, the legal ground being relied on, and the rules of the specific jurisdiction. In many situations a landlord must follow statutory notice and court procedure before lawful eviction. Unlawful eviction rules can also apply if a landlord removes a tenant without the required process.
