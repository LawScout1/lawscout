SAMPLE ANSWER

Landlord eviction example

In many cases, a landlord in England cannot lawfully evict a tenant without following the correct legal process, which may include notice requirements and, in some situations, a court order.

Legal principles

Whether an eviction is lawful depends on the tenancy type, the reason for possession, the notice served, and whether the landlord has complied with the relevant statutory requirements.

Practical next steps

Check the tenancy agreement, identify the notice served, and review whether the landlord has followed the formal possession route. If the matter is urgent, professional advice may be needed.

Important limitation

This example is general information only and not legal advice. Housing law can depend heavily on facts, jurisdiction, and recent legal changes.
