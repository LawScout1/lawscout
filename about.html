About LawScout

LawScout is an AI-powered legal information platform designed to help people understand legal issues more clearly and confidently.

Our goal is to make legal information more accessible, understandable, and affordable by presenting it in plain English and structured formats.

LawScout is intended to help users understand general legal information and identify when they may need professional support. It is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice.

Our Mission

LawScout’s mission is to make legal information accessible, understandable, and affordable through intelligent technology, while guiding users toward the right professional support when needed.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading platform people use to understand the law before speaking to a lawyer.
